ERUMAG Issue 2

Understanding the trend in fertility rates and underlying demographic economics.

ERUMAG Issue 2 is an analytical deep dive written entirely by the TEDU Economics Research Union, highlighting demographic shifts that profoundly affect long-term macroeconomic stability.

"Despite the incentives applied, the downward trend in fertility rates has not been overcome."

Highlights

Demographic Analysis: Comprehensive review of falling fertility rates globally and locally.

Policy Effectiveness: Evaluation of state incentives designed to boost population growth and why they often fall short.

Global Perspectives: Comparing Turkey's demographic trajectory with those of developed and developing nations.
