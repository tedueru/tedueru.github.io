About TEDU ERU

A hub for critical thinking, research, and economic dialogue.

Mission

The mission of TEDU Economic Research Union (ERU) is to waken up academic curiosity for the field of Economics and to provide an opportunity for everyone who wants to progress towards this goal by conducting more profound research and expanding their academic knowledge and consciousness on Economics.

To contribute to economic literacy on current and popular economic topics even for the participants who are from different disciplines as public service.

To bring together the works of both faculty members within the faculty and of the external academicians and researchers with TED University's members.

To benefit both TED University's advancing academic recognition and participants' academic development.

Vision

To be a social economic community that assists the objective, effective, and extensive Economics research by creating an environment full of science, rational ideas, and sophisticated opinions on Economics with the relevant dialectic interpretations of the scientific works and studies.
